My Journey with Cursor AI 🎢

Day 1
🤩

The Honeymoon Phase: "OMG! This is amazing! AI is doing everything!" Pure WOW effect. I felt like Tony Stark with JARVIS.

Day 2
🤔

Reality Check: "Wait... why is it hallucinating?" Learned that mega-prompts don't work. Sometimes AI gets too creative for its own good!

Day 2-3
😅

Frontend Struggles: "I'm not a frontend dev!" React fought back harder than expected. Lesson learned: know your tools before you start!

Day 3
🎨

Design Drama: Tried to recreate EPAM style. Result: "It looks like EPAM's cousin who went to art school and dropped out." 😂

Testing
🧪

Solo QA Life: "I am the user, I am the tester, I am the critic." Test-first approach would've saved my sanity!

Pro Tip
🎭

Multiple Personalities: Used different chat tabs for Product Owner, QA Engineer, UX Designer. No developer tab though... I WAS the developer! 😎

Night Shift
🌙

Code Cleanup: Late night refactoring session. Nothing like cleaning code at 2 AM with your AI buddy!

Reality
📧

Practical Solutions: SignalR was being difficult, so... mailto links it is! Sometimes simple solutions are the best solutions.
